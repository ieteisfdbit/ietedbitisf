About Us

We are awesome TEAM

The core team of IETE branch consists of dedicated individuals who organize workshops, events, and competitions to create an engaging environment for members, fostering technical knowledge, professional growth, and networking opportunities.


IETE is India's leading professional society dedicated to advancing the science and technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, and IT. It serves over 1,25,000 members through various centers, conducts technical events, publishes journals, and provides continuing education and career opportunities. Recognized by the Indian government, IETE plays a crucial role in national development and promotes engineering and technical education for human resource development.



What makes us different ?

The core team of a student chapter is a dedicated group that leads and manages chapter activities, fostering a positive impact on the student community by inspiring and empowering members to explore their interests, develop skills, and contribute to collective growth and success.

Benefits of joining IETE

Leadership Opportunities

Student chapters offer numerous leadership roles and responsibilities to their members. Serving on the core team or taking up other leadership positions allows students to enhance their leadership abilities, decision-making skills, and organizational management skills. These experiences can be valuable in their personal and professional growth.

Industry Exposure

Many student chapters collaborate with industry partners, inviting professionals to deliver guest lectures, organize industry visits, and facilitate internships or job placements. This exposure to the industry gives members insights into real-world applications, current trends, and industry best practices, preparing them for their future careers.

Networking Opportunities

Student chapters offer excellent networking opportunities for members. Students can connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and experts in their field of interest. This networking can lead to valuable connections, mentorship opportunities, internships, and future collaborations.

Skill Development

Student chapters provide a conducive environment for skill development. Through workshops, seminars, competitions, and hands-on projects, members can acquire new skills and enhance their existing ones. These practical learning experiences help students develop their technical, leadership, communication, and teamwork skills, making them more competent and marketable.

Recognition & Awards

Student chapters often participate in competitions and events organized by the parent organization or other institutions. Winning awards and accolades not only brings recognition to the chapter but also boosts the confidence and motivation of its members. It showcases their talents and achievements on a broader platform.
